CNI Challenge

Participants will be asked to design a classification framework that can predict subject diagnosis (ADHD vs. Neurotypical Control) based on brain connectivity data. We will provide 120 examples of each class for training and validation.

CNI Workshop

For the 3rd year, CNI will feature a single-track workshop with keynote speakers, technical paper presentations and poster sessions. In conjunction, CNI will host a Challenge with collaborators at the Kennedy Krieger Institute in Baltimore, MD, USA.
